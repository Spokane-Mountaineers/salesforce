# Create a Nomination Request

This guide walks through creating and managing a Spokane Mountaineers call for nominations using the Election Manager web app.

You will need:

- Access to the [Election Manager](https://script.google.com/a/macros/spokanemountaineers.org/s/AKfycbxKvex6OsXOa-QdXvNpJYn5KfjTdHVb1t6RB3FTieeOlbS5JlMcXxULkWM95hl6EkAB/exec) (requires a spokanemountaineers.org Google account)

---

## Step 1 — Open the Election Manager

Open the [Election Manager](https://script.google.com/a/macros/spokanemountaineers.org/s/AKfycbxKvex6OsXOa-QdXvNpJYn5KfjTdHVb1t6RB3FTieeOlbS5JlMcXxULkWM95hl6EkAB/exec). You will see options to Create New or Manage Existing.

Under **Create New**, click **New Nomination Request**.

---

## Step 2 — Fill in nomination details

Three fields appear at the top of the form:

**Title** — Enter a descriptive name, such as `2026 Annual Nominations`. This becomes the name of the Google Form and the response spreadsheet.

**Start Date** — The date nominations open.

**Close Date** — The last day members can submit a nomination.

**Save To Folder** — Choose the year folder where the files will be stored (e.g., `2026`). If the year folder does not exist yet, select **+ Create New Folder...** and type the folder name.

---

## Step 3 — Build the nomination form

The roles section lists standard preset positions (President, Vice President, Secretary, Treasurer, Board of Directors, Conservation Chair, Paddling Chair).

Check the box next to each position that you want to solicit nominations for.

When you check a position, its details will expand:

- **Role Description** — A pre-filled description that will be shown to members. You can edit this text directly to update it for the current year.
- **Term** — e.g. "1 year"
- **Max Terms** — e.g. "2 consecutive"
- **Time Commit.** — e.g. "10-12 hours per month"

_Note: All preset descriptions automatically include a note reminding members to provide an email and phone number for the nominee so staff can validate them._

**Adding Custom Positions (Write-ins)**
If you are seeking nominations for a role that is not listed (e.g., a new Activity Chair), click **+ Add Custom Position**. You can then type the Position Name, Description, and term details from scratch.

---

## Step 4 — Create the form

Click **Create Nomination Form**.

The app will:

1. Create a Google Form with paragraph text fields for each selected role, including the descriptions and term details.
2. Create a Google Spreadsheet to store responses.
3. Move both files into the year folder you selected.

When it finishes, the links to the form and response sheet will appear. Since nominations are public and don't require secure single-use tokens, you can copy the **View Form** link and distribute it via email newsletters, social media, or the website.

---

## Editing a nomination form

If you need to correct a role description, add a role, or remove a role, you can edit the form directly through the app.

Open the [Election Manager](https://script.google.com/a/macros/spokanemountaineers.org/s/AKfycbxKvex6OsXOa-QdXvNpJYn5KfjTdHVb1t6RB3FTieeOlbS5JlMcXxULkWM95hl6EkAB/exec), click **Manage Election or Nomination**, select your nomination from the dropdown (it will be labeled with `(Nomination)`), and click **Load**. Make your changes to the checked roles and descriptions, and click **Save Nomination Changes**.

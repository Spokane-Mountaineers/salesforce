# Getting Started

New to the Spokane Mountaineers website? This page walks you through your first
visit: activating your account, signing in, and setting up your profile so you can
keep up with the activities you care about.

## Activate your account

Your account is created when you join or renew through Donorbox. After that, you'll
get an activation email with a link to set your password and finish your login.
Follow the link, choose a password, and you're in.

If you joined but never got an activation email, check your spam folder first, then
email [membership@spokanemountaineers.org](mailto:membership@spokanemountaineers.org)
and we'll resend it.

## Sign in

Your username is your **email address with `.smi` added to the end** — for example,
`you@example.com.smi`. If your membership email is a Google or Microsoft address,
you can sign in with one click instead of a password. See [Logging In](logging-in.md)
for the details and for what to do if you're locked out.

## Set up your profile and settings

The first thing to do after you sign in is review your profile and settings. Open
the menu under the **down arrow to the right of your name** in the upper-right
corner of the site. It contains:

- Home
- My Profile
- My Settings
- My Messages
- My Account
- Contact Support
- Logout

Start with **My Settings** to choose what other members can see — new accounts hide
your email and phone by default — and to turn on the email notifications that keep
you in the loop. Then fill out **My Profile** with your activity interests. The full
walkthrough is on [Profile, Settings & Privacy](profile-settings-privacy.md).

## Join the activities you care about

Take a few minutes to find the **Activity Groups** you want to follow and join them.
Each group has its own events, posts, and files, and you choose how often you hear
from it. See [Events & Activity Groups](events-and-activity-groups.md).

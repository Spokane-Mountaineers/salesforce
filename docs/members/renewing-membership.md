# Renewing Your Membership

Renewals go through **Donorbox**, the same service you used to join. The single most
important thing to get right: **renew with your email address, not your website
username.**

!!! danger "Use your email, never your `.smi` username"
    Donorbox matches you by email. If you renew using your website username (your
    email with `.smi` on the end), Donorbox can't match you to your existing record —
    it creates a brand-new contact and breaks your membership history. Always renew
    with your plain email address.

## Keep your details consistent

If you're renewing manually, use the **same name, address, and phone number** you
used before. Small differences create duplicate records — renewing as "David" when
you originally joined as "Dave," for example, can create a second contact.

Not sure what you used? Check your Donorbox receipt, or open **My Profile** on the
website, where you can see the name, email, and phone on file. **My Account** shows
the address.

!!! note "Watch the email on your payment method"
    Some payment tools attach a different email than the one you use for membership.
    Before you finish, make sure the email tied to your membership doesn't get
    replaced by a payment-account email.

## Where to renew

Open **My Account** from the menu under your name for a link to renew. You can renew
your own membership there, but you can't renew another member of your household from
your account — each member renews from their own.

If anything looks off after you renew, email
[membership@spokanemountaineers.org](mailto:membership@spokanemountaineers.org) and
we'll sort it out.

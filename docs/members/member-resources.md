# Member Resources

Beyond events, your membership comes with documents, discounts, and a full slate of
schools and clinics. Most of it lives under two menus on the main site:
**Resources** and **Schools & Clinics**.

## Trip waiver and liability release

From the main menu, choose **Resources → Member Resources**. That page holds the
club's important member documents, and the **trip waiver / liability release** is the
first one on the list. You'll find other documents and links there too.

Some activity groups also post the waiver in their own group files, or link back to
this same document.

## Member discounts

From the main menu, choose **Resources → Member Discounts** for the current list of
discounts available to members. Two pointers:

- To use a discount in person, be ready to show proof of current membership by
  logging into your account on your phone.
- Online discounts may use a discount code instead.

## Schools and Clinics

From the main menu, choose **Schools & Clinics** to see the club's educational
offerings — everything from Backpack School and Rock School to Wilderness First Aid
and Leadership Development.

A program may still show details from its last session until we begin advertising the
next one, so check back as the season approaches. Schools and clinics are often
listed in the Calendar of Events as well.

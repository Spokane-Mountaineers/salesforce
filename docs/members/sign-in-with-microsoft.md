# Sign in with Microsoft

Prefer Microsoft? If the email on your membership is a Microsoft account — work,
school, or a personal `@outlook.com`, `@live.com`, or `@hotmail.com` address — you
can sign in with it instead of a password.

## How it works

When you click **Continue with Microsoft** on the login page:

1. Microsoft verifies who you are.
2. We match you to your existing membership account by email.
3. You're signed in.

Like Google sign-in, this only works for members who already have an account. It
doesn't create a new membership.

## For existing members

1. Click **Continue with Microsoft** on the login page.
2. Choose your Microsoft account.
3. You're in.

## For new members

If you don't have a membership yet, you'll see a message pointing you to:

- [Join through Donorbox](https://donorbox.org/spokanemountaineers-membership-2) if
  you're new.
- [membership@spokanemountaineers.org](mailto:membership@spokanemountaineers.org) if
  you already have an account but sign-in isn't working.

## If something goes wrong

- **Use your regular login.** Your username and password still work — see
  [Logging In](logging-in.md).
- **Try Google instead.** If you also have a Google address on file, it works the
  same way: [Sign in with Google](sign-in-with-google.md).
- **Contact support.** Email
  [membership@spokanemountaineers.org](mailto:membership@spokanemountaineers.org).

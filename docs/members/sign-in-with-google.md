# Sign in with Google

Many members already use a Google account every day, so you can sign in to the
Spokane Mountaineers website with it — no separate password to remember.

## How it works

When you click **Sign in with Google** on the login page:

1. Google verifies who you are.
2. We match you to your existing membership account by email.
3. You're signed in.

Sign in with Google only works for members who already have an account. It doesn't
create a new membership, so your account has to be set up through Donorbox first.

## For existing members

1. Click **Sign in with Google** on the login page.
2. Choose your Google account.
3. You're in.

## For new members

If you don't have a membership yet, you'll see a message pointing you to:

- [Join through Donorbox](https://donorbox.org/spokanemountaineers-membership-2) if
  you're new.
- [membership@spokanemountaineers.org](mailto:membership@spokanemountaineers.org) if
  you already have an account but sign-in isn't working.

## If something goes wrong

- **Use your regular login.** Your username and password still work — see
  [Logging In](logging-in.md).
- **Contact support.** Email
  [membership@spokanemountaineers.org](mailto:membership@spokanemountaineers.org)
  and we'll help link your Google account.

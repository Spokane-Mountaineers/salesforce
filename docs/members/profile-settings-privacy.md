# Profile, Settings & Privacy

Everything about your account lives under the **down arrow to the right of your
name** in the upper-right corner of the site. Click it to open your account menu:
Home, My Profile, My Settings, My Messages, My Account, Contact Support, and
Logout.

Here's what each one does.

## My Profile

A summary of your profile, along with every post you've made in the club's nine
primary groups. From here you can edit:

- Your preferred contact method
- Your activity interests
- Additional information about you
- When you joined the club

## My Settings

This is where you manage your contact information and, importantly, **what other
members can see when they look you up**.

!!! warning "New accounts hide your email and phone by default"
    For every new member, email and phone start out private. If you want other
    members to be able to reach you, change those fields to **Member**. Messaging
    through the site can't be turned off, so members can always message you even
    when your email and phone are hidden.

My Settings is also where you manage **email notifications**. Notifications must be
turned on here for you to receive emails from your activity groups, and there are
extra options to fine-tune what reaches your inbox.

## My Messages

Messages other members have sent you, and where you send your own. When you have
unread messages, a red circle with a number shows how many are waiting.

## My Account

The status of your membership and your household:

- Who the primary contact is
- Billing address and membership information
- Payments made by members of your household
- A link to renew

You can renew your own membership here, but you can't renew another member of your
household from your account — that has to be done from theirs. For how to renew
without breaking your record, see [Renewing Your Membership](renewing-membership.md).

## Contact Support

A member lookup tool for messaging any current member, plus a direct line to club
support at [membership@spokanemountaineers.org](mailto:membership@spokanemountaineers.org).

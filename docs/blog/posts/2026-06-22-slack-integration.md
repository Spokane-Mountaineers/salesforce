# Connect on Slack: New Member Workspace Integration

We are excited to announce that we have launched a new Spokane Mountaineers Slack workspace! Active members can now connect, chat, and coordinate trips in real time. 

To keep our community secure, the workspace is fully integrated with our website login.

<!-- more -->

Our Slack workspace is located at:  
[spokanemountaineers.slack.com](https://spokanemountaineers.slack.com/)

### How it works

We've set up Salesforce as our Single Sign-On (SSO) Identity Provider (IdP) for Slack. This means:

*   **No new passwords:** You log in using your existing Spokane Mountaineers website account credentials.
*   **One-click access:** If you are already signed into the club website on your browser, you'll be logged straight into Slack with a single click.
*   **Active members only:** Access is automatically verified. As long as your membership is active, you have full access to the workspace.

### What is Slack?

Slack is a messaging app that lets us communicate in real time. We have channels for all kinds of outdoor activities and club interests. Here are just a few examples of what you'll find when you join:

*   **`#general`**: For general chat, announcements, and welcoming new members.
*   **Outdoor Activities**: Dedicated channels like `#hiking`, `#climbing`, `#skiing`, and `#biking` where members discuss routes, trail conditions, and plan outings.
*   **Club Committees & Operations**: Channels like `#outreach` and `#ecomm` to get involved with club projects and operations.

There are many more channels to explore once you get signed in!

### Get started

Ready to join? Read our step-by-step [Slack Member Guide](../../members/slack.md) for full instructions on signing in on your computer or phone, finding channels, and troubleshooting.

See you in the workspace!

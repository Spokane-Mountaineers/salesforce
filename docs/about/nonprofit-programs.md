# Nonprofit Programs & Affiliations

The Spokane Mountaineers is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. As such, we are eligible for various nonprofit programs and grants that provide us with essential tools and infrastructure at reduced or no cost.

This page tracks our current affiliations, the benefits we receive, and the status of pending applications.

## Active Programs

### Google for Nonprofits

Google provides us with a full suite of productivity tools that power our official communications and document storage.

- **Benefits**:
    - **Google Workspace**: Custom email addresses (@spokanemountaineers.org) for board members and committees.
    - **Shared Drives**: Centralized storage for club records and assets.
- **Administration**: Managed via the [Google Admin Console](https://admin.google.com).
- **Key Contact**: Web Development Team (`webdev@spokanemountaineers.org`).

### Microsoft for Nonprofits

Microsoft offers a variety of grants and discounted software to help nonprofits scale their impact.

- **Benefits**:
    - **Azure Grant**: We receive a **$2,000 annual credit** for Azure services. This is currently used to host our website and related infrastructure.
    - **Microsoft 365**: Eligibility for donated or heavily discounted M365 Business Premium licenses.
- **Administration**: Managed via the [Microsoft Nonprofit Portal](https://nonprofit.microsoft.com).
- **Usage**: Credits are applied to our Azure Subscription. Monitoring is required to ensure we stay within the grant limit.

    - Unlimited message history and integrations.

## Administrative Reference

### TechSoup

[TechSoup](https://www.techsoup.org/) is the primary validation service used by almost all major technology providers (Google, Microsoft, Slack, etc.) to verify nonprofit status.

- **Validation Token**: Often required when applying for new programs.
- **Account Access**: Access is limited to the Treasurer and the Web Development lead.

### Required Documentation

When applying for new programs, the following documents are typically required:

1.  **IRS Determination Letter**: Confirming our 501(c)(3) status.
2.  **Form 990**: Our most recent tax filing.
3.  **Bylaws**: Occasionally requested to verify organizational structure.

For Members¶

Welcome to the Spokane Mountaineers member help center. These guides cover the everyday things you'll do on www.spokanemountaineers.org: signing in, setting up your profile, finding trips, and making the most of your membership.

New here? Start with Getting Started.

  • Getting Started

    Activate your account, sign in, and set up your profile.

  • Logging In

    Your username, password resets, and Google or Microsoft sign-in.

  • Profile, Settings & Privacy

    Control what other members can see and manage your notifications.

  • Events & Activity Groups

    Browse the calendar and join the groups that run the trips.

  • Slack Workspace

    Log in and chat with other members, coordinate trips, and share rides.

  • Member Resources

    The waiver, member discounts, and schools and clinics.

  • Renewing Your Membership

    Renew without breaking your membership record.

Need a hand?¶

Use Contact Support in the menu under your name on the website, or email membership@spokanemountaineers.org.
